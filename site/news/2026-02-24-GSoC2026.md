title: [ANN] Pharo accepted for the GSOC 2026
layout: blogpost
publishDate: "2026-02-24"


We are happy to announce that Pharo Consortium has been selected for Google Summer of Code 2026! This is an exciting opportunity for us to collaborate with talented students on open-source projects throughout the summer.

The contributor application period is March 16 – March 31 (18:00 UTC). Accepted contributors will be announced on April 30, and the coding period will officially start on May 25.

Please check out our website for detailed information about the application process and project ideas:
[https://gsoc.pharo.org](https://gsoc.pharo.org)
